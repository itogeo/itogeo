About Ito Geospatial

Ito Geospatial supports agencies and community groups that need geospatial horsepower without a full internal department. We focus on remote sensing, elevation model cleanup, and pragmatic automation that keeps local data accurate.

Every engagement is hands-on. We scale a core team of full-time staff with rotating specialists, verify every input, and leave behind tools and training that stand up to public review.
