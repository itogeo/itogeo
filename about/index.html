Our Story

Based in Bozeman, Montana, we started Ito Geospatial because we kept seeing the same problem: small governments and organizations needed professional mapping capabilities, but couldn't afford a dedicated GIS department—or got handed tools so complex that only one person knew how to use them.

We build differently. Every workflow is designed to be run by your existing staff. Every map is documented so your board members understand where the data comes from. Every tool is tested by people who aren't GIS experts, because that's who'll be using it day-to-day.

The result? Agencies that can answer their own questions. Utilities that can update their own asset maps. Watershed councils that can show stakeholders exactly what changed and why. We hand over the keys—and stay on call when you need us.
