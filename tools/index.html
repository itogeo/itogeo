Geospatial Tools for Everyone

We're building free, open tools that make professional geospatial work accessible to small teams. No expensive software licenses. No GIS degree required.

Free to Use

Map Digitizer

Turn screenshots of zoning maps, planning documents, or any color-coded map image into georeferenced GeoJSON. Upload an image, set control points to align it with real-world coordinates, and download export-ready data for your GIS software.

Note: This is a free tool—it may take 20-30 seconds to wake up on first load.

Automatic Street Detection

OCR reads street names directly from your map image and finds intersections for georeferencing.

Smart Geocoding

Uses OpenStreetMap to convert detected intersections into real-world coordinates.

Color-Based Zone Detection

HSV analysis identifies zoning areas by color—residential, commercial, industrial, and more.

Multiple Export Formats

Download as GeoJSON, Shapefile, KML, or KMZ for use in any GIS platform.

Montana Airport Economic Impact Study

In partnership with Zenith Economics and the MSU Geospatial Core Facility, we mapped the economic contributions of airports across Montana. This StoryMap blends survey data, aviation infrastructure, and regional workforce trends to keep legislators and funding partners aligned.

  • Statewide airport inventory tied to passenger counts and capital investments
  • Economic impact rollups that translate spatial analysis into policy-ready briefs
  • ArcGIS StoryMap delivery so rural and urban stakeholders share the same narrative
