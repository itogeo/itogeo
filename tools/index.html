Geospatial Tools for Everyone

We're building free, open tools that make professional geospatial work accessible to small teams. No expensive software licenses. No GIS degree required.

Free to Use

Map Digitizer

Turn screenshots of zoning maps, planning documents, or any color-coded map image into georeferenced GeoJSON. Upload an image, let the tool detect streets and intersections automatically, and download export-ready data for your GIS software.

Automatic Street Detection

OCR reads street names directly from your map image and finds intersections for georeferencing.

Smart Geocoding

Uses OpenStreetMap to convert detected intersections into real-world coordinates.

Color-Based Zone Detection

HSV analysis identifies zoning areas by color—residential, commercial, industrial, and more.

Multiple Export Formats

Download as GeoJSON, Shapefile, KML, or KMZ for use in any GIS platform.
